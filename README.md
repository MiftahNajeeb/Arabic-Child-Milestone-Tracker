# Arabic Child Milestone Tracker

A free, Arabic-first mobile app that helps parents track their child's early development and catch potential delays early, when intervention matters most.

## The Problem

In Libya and across the Arab world, parents who suspect a developmental issue in their child, such as a sign of autism, a speech delay, or another condition, have almost no reliable, accessible way to check early.

In the United States, the **CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention)** built a free, medically-reviewed app that millions of parents use to track child development. Every milestone in it was reviewed by medical and child development experts. However, it exists only in English and Spanish. No Arabic app currently matches its accuracy or functionality.

## The Solution

This app helps Arabic-speaking parents track their child's development across key areas:

- 🗣️ **Language & speech**
- 🏃 **Movement & motor skills**
- 🧠 **Learning & cognition**
- 👶 **Social & emotional development**

Through simple, interactive, culturally adapted checklists, it tells parents when something may need attention, guides them on when to act early, and connects them to professional support.

## Key Features (Planned)

- Fully Arabic interface with right-to-left (RTL) support
- Age-based developmental milestone checklists
- Early-warning flags for potential delays
- Guidance and next steps for parents
- Offline support for low-connectivity areas
- Connection to local healthcare resources

## Status

Completed:

Next steps:

To give every Arabic-speaking parent a trusted tool to support their child's healthiest possible start in life.
